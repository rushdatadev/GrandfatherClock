Clock face library

Eight dial variations as static SVG resource files. Each variation lives in clocks/<variation>/ with four files: dial.svg, hour.svg, minute.svg, second.svg. All are 1024×1024 with rotation pivot at the center (512, 512). Stack the hands on top of the dial and apply transform: rotate().
